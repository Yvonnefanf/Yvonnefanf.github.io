Yifan Zhang

 

I am Yifan ZHANG. I am currently a ph.D. student at SoC, National University of Singapore, working in the UbiComp Lab under the supervision of Prof. Brian Lim.
My research interests include Explainable Artificial Intelligence(XAI) and Human-Computer Interaction(HCI).

Specifically, I focus on designing and developing interpretable machine learning (ML) models and explainable AI techniques to enhance the transparency of complex decision-making and prediction processes. By applying human-centered principles, I strive to provide explanations that are cognitively friendly, thereby improving overall interpretability.
